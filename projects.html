rapitup

Ruby on Rails, Delayed::Job

The Internet's youngest battle-rap platform.

The funny thing is, you can never predict when or where a rap battle will take place when you're on the web. One could begin right after you log into Facebook, or over Twitter in the form of 140-character-long subtweets directed at you. Point being, you never know. And even though it's the sheer randomness of the rapping that makes it all the more fun, I wondered if it would be possible to capitalize on the phenomenon by giving it a home.

Anyway, on rapitup you can: write raps, respond to raps, win some, lose some, upvote, downvote — all from behind a computer screen. Cool, huh?
