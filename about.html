ABOUT ME

The Art of Peer Pressure

Hey, I’m Michael Marek, a rap-obsessed professional dreamer, who also happens to be a software engineering student at the University of Waterloo!

Don’t be fooled by the occasional colloquialism however – I also talk technology: casually and seriously. Although I can only speak of gaming in a somewhat past-tense, nostalgic sense, I sure won’t hesitate to engage in an insightful and relevant conversation regarding, say, Java, HTML, CSS, JS, Python, or even C.

Some of my other interests include gymnastics, tennis, pop culture in general and, predictably, music. As much as I'd like to play and compose myself, I mainly just listen to it and do research on the industry and its artists. (I am a frequent tracker of the Billboard music charts.)

Tying into how I like analyzing songs and whatnot, I frequently edit and annotate on Genius.

Until I can think of something else to say about myself, might I interest you in a light read?

'Cause I've got a blog that should provide you with just that.

You may also want to take a gander at my LinkedIn page or resume.
