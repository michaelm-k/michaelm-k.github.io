About Me

The Art of Peer Pressure

Hey, I’m Michael Marek, a rap-obsessed professional dreamer, who also happens to be a software engineering student at the University of Waterloo (class of 2019)!

As with many, my earliest exposure to programming was in high school, where I took two courses in Java. After focusing a lot on algorithms, OO programming, and solving CCC-style questions, I took an interest in front end web development. That motivated me to learn HTML, CSS, and JS in the summer of '13 and build this site, which I routinely worked on before I finally launched it in the winter. Since then, I've broadened my horizon to include a little back end too (thanks to Django) in addition to more object-oriented stuff in C and C++.

When I'm not programming, I tend to be editing or annotating something on Genius (check out my 'tates :D), doing a little research on the state of the music industry, or getting my blogging game on.

Until I can think of something else to say about myself, feel free to browse around!
